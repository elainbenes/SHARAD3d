The data used here can be downloaded from http://pds-geosciences.wustl.edu/mro/mro-m-sharad-5-radargram-v1/mrosh_2001/data/

Both geodata and imgdata should be downloaded from geom/ and rgram/ respectively. 
For example, the data with product ID of 00220201 should be downloaded from
http://pds-geosciences.wustl.edu/mro/mro-m-sharad-5-radargram-v1/mrosh_2001/data/geom/s_0022xx/s_00220201_geom.lbl
http://pds-geosciences.wustl.edu/mro/mro-m-sharad-5-radargram-v1/mrosh_2001/data/geom/s_0022xx/s_00220201_geom.tab
http://pds-geosciences.wustl.edu/mro/mro-m-sharad-5-radargram-v1/mrosh_2001/data/rgram/s_0022xx/s_00220201_rgram.lbl
http://pds-geosciences.wustl.edu/mro/mro-m-sharad-5-radargram-v1/mrosh_2001/data/rgram/s_0022xx/s_00220201_rgram.img
